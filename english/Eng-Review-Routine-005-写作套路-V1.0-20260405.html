写作套路 ​

结构清晰→内容充实→语言高级

一、题型识别 ​

高考写作题型 ​

题型分值字数特点
应用文15分80-100词书信、通知、演讲稿等
读后续写20分150词续写故事，情节合理
概要写作20分60词概括要点

二、应用文写作 ​

2.1 书信类 ​

建议信 ​

结构：

开头：表明写信目的
主体：具体建议（分点）
结尾：希望采纳，期待回复

模板：

Dear [Name],

I am writing to offer some suggestions on [topic].

First of all, I suggest that you [suggestion 1]. This will help you [benefit 1]. 

Besides, it would be a good idea to [suggestion 2]. By doing so, you can [benefit 2].

Finally, I recommend that you [suggestion 3]. I am sure this will [benefit 3].

I hope you will find these suggestions helpful. Looking forward to your reply.

Yours sincerely,
Li Hua

常用句型：

  • I am writing to offer some suggestions on...
  • I suggest that you (should)...
  • It would be a good idea to...
  • I recommend that...
  • I hope you will find these suggestions helpful.

申请信 ​

结构：

开头：自我介绍+申请目的
主体：申请理由（能力、经验、动机）
结尾：表达期待，附上联系方式

模板：

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am Li Hua, a student from [school]. I am writing to apply for [position/opportunity].

I believe I am qualified for this position for the following reasons. First, I have [ability/experience 1]. Second, I am good at [ability 2]. Most importantly, I am enthusiastic about [topic] and eager to [goal].

I would appreciate it if you could give me this opportunity. Please contact me at [phone number] or [email].

Looking forward to your reply.

Yours sincerely,
Li Hua

常用句型：

  • I am writing to apply for...
  • I believe I am qualified for...
  • I would appreciate it if you could...
  • Please contact me at...

感谢信 ​

模板：

Dear [Name],

I am writing to express my sincere gratitude for [what you did].

Your help/kindness has [impact]. Without your help, I would not have been able to [achievement]. I was deeply touched by [specific detail].

Thanks again for your generosity. I hope I can do something for you in return.

Yours sincerely,
Li Hua

2.2 通知/演讲稿 ​

通知 ​

格式：

Notice

[内容：时间、地点、事件、要求]

[落款]
[日期]

模板：

Notice

A [event] will be held [time] at [place]. All [people] are welcome to attend.

The event will include [activities]. Please [requirements].

For more information, please contact [contact person] at [phone number].

[Organization]
[Date]

演讲稿 ​

结构：

开头：问候+引入话题
主体：论点+论据
结尾：总结+感谢

模板：

Dear teachers and fellow students,

It is my great honor to stand here and give a speech on [topic].

As we all know, [background]. However, [problem]. Therefore, [your opinion].

First, [argument 1]. Second, [argument 2]. Last but not least, [argument 3].

In conclusion, [summary]. Let's [call to action].

Thank you for listening!

三、读后续写 ​

3.1 解题步骤 ​

  1. 读懂原文：把握人物、情节、冲突
  2. 分析段落首句：确定续写方向
  3. 构思情节：合理、连贯、有高潮
  4. 写作：注意语言生动

3.2 情节设计原则 ​

  • 合理：符合生活逻辑
  • 连贯：与原文衔接自然
  • 积极：传递正能量
  • 完整：有起因、经过、结果

3.3 语言技巧 ​

描写情感：

  • Tears welled up in her eyes.
  • His heart sank.
  • A wave of relief washed over her.

描写动作：

  • Without hesitation, he rushed to...
  • She froze, unable to move.

环境烘托：

  • The wind howled outside.
  • The sun broke through the clouds.

四、高分语言技巧 ​

4.1 高级句型 ​

定语从句：

As is known to all, reading is beneficial to us.
Those who read regularly tend to have better grades.

非谓语动词：

Faced with difficulties, we should never give up.
Having finished his homework, he went out to play.

倒装句：

Only in this way can we solve the problem.
Not only does he study hard, but he also helps others.

强调句：

It is reading that broadens our horizons.

虚拟语气：

If I were you, I would take this opportunity.
Without your help, I would have failed.

4.2 高级词汇替换 ​

普通词高级词
importantsignificant, essential, vital, crucial
goodbeneficial, favorable, excellent
badharmful, unfavorable, negative
thinkbelieve, consider, maintain, argue
veryextremely, remarkably, exceedingly
more and morean increasing number of, increasingly
helpassist, contribute to, be beneficial to
useutilize, make use of, take advantage of
want tointend to, desire to, be eager to
because ofdue to, owing to, thanks to

4.3 过渡词 ​

递进：

  • First, ... Second, ... Finally, ...
  • Besides, In addition, What's more, Furthermore

转折：

  • However, Nevertheless, On the contrary, While

因果：

  • Therefore, Thus, As a result, Consequently

举例：

  • For example, For instance, Such as

总结：

  • In conclusion, To sum up, All in all, In short

五、检查清单 ​

内容检查 ​

  • [ ] 格式正确（书信有称呼落款）
  • [ ] 要点齐全
  • [ ] 逻辑清晰
  • [ ] 字数合适

语言检查 ​

  • [ ] 时态一致
  • [ ] 主谓一致
  • [ ] 拼写正确
  • [ ] 标点正确

提升检查 ​

  • [ ] 有高级句型（从句、非谓语、倒装）
  • [ ] 有高级词汇
  • [ ] 有过渡词
  • [ ] 书写工整
