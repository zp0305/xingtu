一、本章地图 ​

核心目标 ​

学完本章，你将：

  1. 掌握非谓语动词的用法（不定式、动名词、分词）
  2. 熟练运用各类从句（定语从句、名词性从句、状语从句）
  3. 理解特殊句式（倒装、强调、虚拟语气）
  4. 能够分析长难句结构

二、非谓语动词 ⭐ ​

2.1 不定式 (to do) ​

作主语：

To learn English well is important.
= It is important to learn English well.（形式主语）

作宾语：

I want to go home.
I decide to study abroad.

作宾补：

I want you to help me.
The teacher told us to finish the homework.

作定语：

I have a lot of work to do.
He is always the first to come.

作状语：

目的：I came here to see you.
结果：He woke up to find everyone gone.
原因：I'm glad to see you.

省略to的情况：

  • 使役动词：make/let/have sb. do sth.
  • 感官动词：see/watch/hear/notice sb. do sth.

2.2 动名词 (doing) ​

作主语：

Swimming is good exercise.
Reading aloud is helpful.

作宾语（接动名词的动词）：

enjoy doing 喜欢做
finish doing 完成做
practise doing 练习做
consider doing 考虑做
suggest doing 建议做
avoid doing 避免做
keep doing 持续做
mind doing 介意做

作表语：

My hobby is reading.
His job is teaching.

2.3 分词 ​

现在分词 (doing)：

  • 主动、进行
  • 作定语：a sleeping baby
  • 作状语：Walking in the park, I met an old friend.
  • 作宾补：I found him reading in the library.

过去分词 (done)：

  • 被动、完成
  • 作定语：a broken cup
  • 作状语：Seen from the hill, the city looks beautiful.
  • 作宾补：I had my hair cut yesterday.

2.4 非谓语动词选择判断 ​

情况形式
表目的to do
表进行/主动doing
表完成/被动done
介词后doing

三、从句 ⭐ ​

3.1 定语从句 ​

关系代词：

先行词主语宾语定语
人who/thatwho/whom/that/省略whose
物which/thatwhich/that/省略whose/of which

关系副词：

  • when（表时间）
  • where（表地点）
  • why（表原因）

注意：

  • that不能引导非限制性定语从句
  • 介词后指人用whom，指物用which

3.2 名词性从句 ​

主语从句：

What he said is true.
Whether he will come is uncertain.

宾语从句：

I know that he is right.
I don't know what he wants.

表语从句：

The problem is that we don't have enough time.

同位语从句：

The news that he won the game excited us.
（that不省略，只起连接作用）

3.3 状语从句 ​

类型连词
时间when, while, as, before, after, since, until
地点where, wherever
原因because, since, as
条件if, unless, as long as
让步although, though, even if
目的so that, in order that
结果so...that, such...that

四、特殊句式 ⭐ ​

4.1 倒装句 ​

完全倒装（谓语全部提前）：

Here comes the bus.
Out rushed the children.

部分倒装（助动词提前）：

否定词开头：Never have I seen such a beautiful place.
Only开头：Only in this way can we solve the problem.
So...that：So fast does he run that I can't catch up.

4.2 强调句 ​

结构：It is/was + 被强调部分 + that/who...

原句：I met Tom in the park yesterday.
强调主语：It was I who/that met Tom in the park yesterday.
强调宾语：It was Tom who/that I met in the park yesterday.
强调地点：It was in the park that I met Tom yesterday.
强调时间：It was yesterday that I met Tom in the park.

4.3 虚拟语气 ​

与现在相反：

If I were you, I would take the job.
（从句：did/were，主句：would/could/should/might do）

与过去相反：

If I had known the truth, I would have told you.
（从句：had done，主句：would/could/should/might have done）

与将来相反：

If I should/were to see him, I would tell him.
（从句：should do/were to do，主句：would do）

五、长难句分析 ​

分析步骤 ​

  1. 找谓语动词（确定句子数量）
  2. 找连词（确定从句类型）
  3. 划分主干（主谓宾/主系表）
  4. 识别修饰成分（定语、状语、同位语）

示例分析 ​

例句：

The book that I borrowed from the library yesterday is very interesting.

分析：

  • 谓语1：borrowed（定语从句中）
  • 谓语2：is（主句）
  • 主干：The book is very interesting.
  • 定语从句：that I borrowed from the library yesterday（修饰book）

翻译：我昨天从图书馆借的那本书很有趣。

六、配套练习 ​

练习1：选择正确的非谓语形式 ​

  1. I enjoy ______ (read) novels.
  2. He told me ______ (finish) the work on time.
  3. ______ (see) from the top, the city is beautiful.

答案：1. reading 2. to finish 3. Seen

练习2：完成句子 ​

  1. 我认识的第一个人是李华。（定语从句） The first person ______ was Li Hua.

  2. 重要的是我们要努力学习。（主语从句） ______ is ______ we study hard.

答案：

  1. (that) I knew
  2. What; that
